The main role of Mati Mitra Foundation is to work for the upliftment of society, sepecially in rural areas, by addressing social, economic, and environmental challanges. The foundation focuses on women empowerment, promoting education, health and livelihood opportunities for women to help them become self-reliant. It also works on rural development by improving access to basic facilities, skill developemnt programs, and suistainable farming practices. The foundation engages in social awareness campaigns, child welfare, environmental conservation, and community development projects. Through the efforts, Mati Mitra Foundation aims to create an inclusive sociery where marginalized communities have equal opportunities to grow and thrive.

Mati Mitra Foundation Events

Health camp

Join us in raising awareness and promoting to end polio for good. At Mati Mitra Foundation, we work to ensure every child enjoys a healthy, safe and polio-free future. Together, we can protect lives and build a stronger, healthier worlds.

Yoga

Spreading awareness about the power of yoga for holistic living. At Mati Mitra Foundation, we encourage people to embrace yoga as a way to achieve physical health, mental peace, and spiritual balance. Together, let's promote wellness and hormony through the timeless practice of yoga.

Career Guidance

Inspiring and mentoring young minds to build successful careers. At Mati Mitra Foundation, we guide students and youth through career counselling, skill development, and motivational sessions to help them discover their potential and achieve their dreams. Together, we are shaping confident and capable future leaders.

Swacchata Abhiyan

Encouraging every hand to take part in the mission for a clean environment. At Mati Mitra Foundation, we actively promote cleanliness drives, waste management awareness, and community participation to create a healthier and greener society. Together, let's take a step towards a cleaner and more suistainable future.

Skill Development

Training individuals to build their skills and confidence. At Mati Mitra Foundation, we provide learning opportunities, workshops, and hands-on training programs to empower people with the skills needed for personal and professional growth.Together, we are creating a pathway toward self-reliance and a brighter future.

Student Interaction

An engaging session by Mati Mitra Foundation to inspire and connect with students through fun activities, learning, and motivation. The initiative aims to encourage creativity, confidence, and positive thinking among young minds, helping them grow personally and academically.

Tree Plantation

A tree Plantation drive to promote a greener environment and create awareness about the importance of trees for a suistainable future. At Mati Mitra Foundtion, we encourage individuals and communities to plant, nurture, and protect trees, contributing to cleaner air, balanced ecosystems and a healthier planet for generations to come.

Good Handwriting

An event focused on encouraging creativity and good handwriting among students through fun writing and drawing activities. At Mati Mitra Foundation, we aim to nurture fine motor skills, concentration and artistic expression in children while making learning enjoyable and rewarding.
